Frequently Answered Questions

Where should I surrender the missing items?

In collaboration with the officials, you can surrender it to them. After that, fill up the form or just let the officials answer it.

Where should I claim a missing item?

Fill up the form first and choose the radio buttom claim item. It is done so to verify first whether you are the owner of the said item. After verification, you will be contacted by the officials.

What informations do I need for answering the form?

Whether you are reporting a lost item or surrendering a lost item, you need to have this information:
  1. Item (For example, an ID, wallet, keys, cellphone, etc.)
  2. Date of when you had lost or find an item
  3. Location of where the item had been lost or found
  4. User's Name
  5. Contact Number
  6. Email
  7. Item Picture (Highly encouraged when reporting a missing item, but optional)

Lost and Found Scope

The FindIt lost and found service would only be available for residents of Brgy. 5, Calamba City, Laguna, Philippines. While the software itself may only be available for the said place, organizations may also request the services provided by FindIt.

FindIt's Privacy Policy

FindIt lost and found service is fully compliant with Republic Act No. 10173, otherwise known as the Data Privacy Act of 2012. FindIt would implement carefully security measures from the information collected form the users and would solely be used to provide its services. Furthermore, informations collected would only be used to further improve FindIt's services. Providing the said information to others would only happen if required by law.

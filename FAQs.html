Frequently Answered Questions

Where should I surrender the missing items?

In collaboration with the officials, you can surrender it to them. After that, fill up the form or just let the officials answer it.

Where should I claim a missing item?

Fill up the form first and choose the radio buttom claim item. It is done so to verify first whether you are the owner of the said item. After verification, you will be contacted by the officials.

FindIt Iskolar


FindIt Iskolar has been established to meet the insufficiencies of lost and found. Now that the world is becoming more reliant on technology, we should maximize it to the fullest and widen the reach of whatever services, products, advocacies, etc. an organization offers to audiences. Losing our items was a big inconvenience to everyone, and it just got worse when we lost important things like wallets, IDs, flash drives, cellphones, etc. Furthermore, finding the missing items would prove to be hard when we lose them in crowded places. The platform was established in the early part of 2024 and has been developed ever since to further polish the system.
